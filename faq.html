    5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
      • You need to go to learn.hz.nl > Facilities > Selfservice portal > Make a Reservation > Reserve meeting room or Project group room or click here
    6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
      • Free parking is available at the car park at Poelendaelesingel 8 in Middelburg. This is at a few minutes walk from HZ.
